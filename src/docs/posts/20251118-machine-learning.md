### Transformation Phases

<div class="clearfix">

**Phase 1 (2022)**: Started as a unified organization where all functions reported through a single structure. This worked well when we were small but became unwieldy as we scaled.

**Phase 2 (2023)**: Split into four distinct departments - Product, Engineering, Sales, and Marketing. Each had autonomy to operate independently and develop specialized expertise.

**Phase 3 (2024)**: Consolidated back to two core units. Sales functions were absorbed into Product for better customer feedback loops, while Marketing was integrated into Engineering to align growth initiatives with technical capabilities.
